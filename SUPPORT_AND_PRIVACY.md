### Common questions

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| **How do I delete my account?** | **Settings** → *Delete Account*. This permanently removes your auth profile, device tokens, friendships, and friend invites; deletes any **friend-only (non-group)** expenses you created; transfers sole-owned groups to another member or deletes the group if you were the only member; and replaces your identity on shared-group expenses and settlements with *Deleted user* so other members’ balances stay correct. | **How do I export my data?** | Settings → Account → *Export data* generates a JSON archive of your profile, groups, expenses, and settlements. |

| **Can I use PandaSplit offline?** | The app caches the last loaded data, but creating expenses, settlements, and friendships requires connectivity to our backend. |
| **How are balances calculated?** | Each expense is split among the selected members; running totals are recomputed server-side from `splits` and `settlements`. See the in-app *How splits work* explainer in Help & Feedback. |
| **I think there's a bug — what now?** | Email support with the info above. Critical bugs (data loss, auth failure) are triaged the same business day. |

## 2. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information PandaSplit
collects, why we collect it, how it's stored, and the choices you have.

### 2.1 Data we collect

PandaSplit only collects what is required to operate the bill-splitting
features you ask for.

| Category | Examples | Source | Why we collect it |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Account identifiers** | Apple user identifier (Sign in with Apple), email address, phone number (optional) | You, at sign-up | Authentication, account recovery, finding friends you already know. |
| **Profile data** | Username, full name, avatar image, default currency, locale | You | Displayed to your friends and group co-members so they can identify you in shared expenses. |
| **Financial-context metadata** | Group names, expense descriptions, amounts, currency, dates, who paid, splits between members, settlements | You and people you share groups with | The core ledger that powers balance calculations. PandaSplit does **not** process payments or connect to your bank. |
| **Engagement signals** | Activity log entries (expense added, friend accepted, etc.) | Generated by the app from your actions | Powers the in-app activity feed and notifications. |
| **Push notification token** | An opaque APNs device token | Apple, after you grant permission | Lets us deliver notifications about new expenses, settlements, and friend requests. |
| **Diagnostic data** | Crash signatures, error logs (no message bodies), backend request timing | Generated automatically | Reliability and debugging. Limited to operational metadata. |

We **do not** collect: precise location, contacts (we never read your
address book), photos beyond an avatar you explicitly choose, advertising
identifiers, browsing history, microphone, or camera input.

### 2.2 How we use the data

- Provide the service: authenticate you, render groups and expenses,
  compute balances, send you notifications you opted into.
- Maintain quality: investigate errors and security incidents, prevent
  abuse, fix bugs.
- Communicate: respond to support requests; send transactional alerts
  (e.g. account/security emails). We do **not** send marketing emails.

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information, and we do **not**
use your data to build advertising profiles.

### 2.4 Sharing with other users

PandaSplit is inherently social. By design:

- Members of a **group** can see the group name, the other members, the
  expenses recorded in that group, and the resulting balances.
- Your **friends** (mutually accepted connections) can see your username,
  full name, avatar, and any shared group expenses.
- Nothing is publicly searchable; there is no public profile page.

You can leave a group at any time; your historical expenses remain part
of that group's ledger so balances stay consistent for other members.

### 2.6 Retention

- **Account-linked data** (profile, groups, expenses, settlements, device
  tokens) is retained while your account is active.
- **Account deletion** (in-app **Settings** → *Delete Account*) deletes your
  Supabase Auth user and profile row, which cascades to device tokens,
  friendships, friend invites, in-app notifications, and your group
  membership rows. **Friend-only (non-group) expenses** you created are
  removed. **Shared-group expenses and settlements** you participated in
  stay in the ledger for other members; references to you are replaced with
  a non-login “Deleted user” sentinel so amounts remain consistent. If you
  were the **sole owner** of a group with other members, ownership passes to
  the longest-tenured remaining member; if you were the only member, the group
  is deleted.
- **Diagnostic logs** are retained for up to **90 days** and then purged.
